IEEE COMPUTER SOCIETY

With nearly 20 student members and 2 professional members, IEEE-VBIT SB has filed a petition to initiate the computer society student chapter. The acknowledgment has been received from the computer society headquarters. The motive to initiate the computer society is to make the computer science and Information technology students to make computing as their profession. New Innovative ideas are being implemented to make students and professionals to get a fruitful result.

Vision

The IEEE-VBIT SB encourages the student members to focus on research and development promoting the establishment of IEEE Student Branches in colleges which does not have an IEEE SB. To Motivate and encourage students to present papers at different technical conferences and participate in the events and competitions conducted by IEEE. Contribute to society by adopting a local school and involvement in charitable events

Mission

The IEEE-VBIT SB promotes technical awareness amongst its college students and helps to cultivate the same interest in the other colleges. The student Branch emphasizes on conducting activities which can develop the technical cognition of a student and also fosters leadership skills.
